About Us


Gaia is an infrastructure & industrial supplier, supplying products all about water, such as: pipe, fittings, valves, hoses, water meters and pipeline ancillary products.
At the heart of Gaia is a focus to treat customers and suppliers with fairness and to always act with integrity.
Gaia is an independent company, owned by its personnel was established for the purpose of providing full procurement and export services to companies around the world.
We are supporting projects in our local communities, invest in development and enhance and support the local communities where we are. Incorporating under one roof all the functions of purchasing, warehousing, crating, shipping and financing, thus providing clients a turnkey service with a single point of contact.
